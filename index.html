The Forgotten Ruins of Skyler

A Minecraft-based ARG uncovering the mystery of Skyler and the "cult" of mages.

Story Clues About

🌨️ Skyler's Origin

When Skyler was a pup, he was abandoned and left wandering a snowy taiga for a year, surviving on salmon and beef. One day, he met 5 mysterious purple-robed mages collecting water samples by the shore...

🧩 Clues & Puzzles

Explore the Minecraft server and search for hidden messages, paintings, and mysterious ruins. Every detail could be a hint.

  • Check inside ruined towers for strange symbols
  • Some paintings may hide ciphers...
  • Look for coded books left behind by the lost mercenaries

🐺 About the ARG

This interactive story takes place in a custom Minecraft world. You play as explorers unraveling the fate of Skyler's descendant — a demi-human hunted by a long-lost mage cult.
